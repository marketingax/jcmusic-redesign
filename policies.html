Enterprise Policies

Guidelines for Professional Partnership & Excellence.

Cancellation & Rescheduling

We value the time and commitment of both our mentors and students. To maintain the highest level of service, we enforce a strict 48-hour notice policy for all cancellations and rescheduling requests.

  • Sessions cancelled with less than 48 hours' notice are non-refundable and forfeited.
  • One emergency rescheduling per 6-month term is permitted without penalty.
  • No-shows result in immediate session forfeiture and no makeup credit.

Income Protection & Refunds

JC Music Enterprise operates on a professional residency model. Our policy is designed to ensure stability and focus for both the business and the student.

  • All deposits and upfront package payments are non-refundable.
  • Starter Foundations packages expire 30 days from the first session.
  • Credit-for-future-services is not transferable between students.

Privacy & Communications

Your privacy is paramount. We do not share student information or payment data with third parties. Automated SMS and Email reminders are used solely for scheduling and enrollment purposes.
