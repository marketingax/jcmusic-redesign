Led by Paul Jerry Compere

With more than 25 years of performing and producing excellence, Paul Jerry Compere brings a world-class pedigree to every student. An alumnus of Westminster Choir College and a Juilliard-affiliated mentor, his work has earned a Peabody Award for the scoring of 'Cops and Robbers'.

Jerry’s credits include collaborations with Lauryn Hill, Wyclef Jean, and Tye Tribbett. This is not just a music lesson—it is a mentorship from a master of the craft.
